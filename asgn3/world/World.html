Blocky World: Cheese Run

ABOUT: -----------------------------
You are controlling this rat, navigate and break through the walls to collect cheese.
You can only break through walls when in First POV mode or the starting mode.
Eat all the cheese on the board as fast as possible!!!
There are always 5 cheeses and there is a leaderboard at the bottom.
Note: sometimes cheese isn't easily visible on the overhead map. (find it or hit Restart for a new arrangement!)

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

XXX


Controls --------------------------

Movement Keys:
W: forward
A: left
S: back
D: right

R: EAT THE CHEESE!!!!!

Camera Rotation:
Q, E, Mouse(click dragging)

Building:
"1": ADD BLOCK
"2": DELETE BLOCK
